ANDI BORDT 2018©

I am a multidisciplinary artist and designer based in Toronto. Between pursuing my Bachelors of Design at OCAD University and maintaining a freelance and private practice — My experimental typography work has been popping up in various city settings.

As of late my work mainly consists of text-based pieces that are strategically placed in contexts where fine art is not usually present, such as industrial buildings, vacant properties, and unmarked doors – appearing in non-gallery settings.


Clientele: KOTN , Untitled&Co , Nightmare, Diptych Studio , Grey93, Moonshine

Past Work: Fancy Boys - Graphic Design Intern - Digital and print-based projects for clients such as:
The Killers, Julian Casablancas, Karen O, Cult Records, Ryan Hemsworth, NXNE, TD Canada Trust, 18Waits, Third Eye Blind, Beau’s Brewery and Equator Coffee .
